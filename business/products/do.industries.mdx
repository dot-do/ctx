## Architecture

### 5-Layer SDK Stack

**Layer 1: Foundation** (Zero Dependencies)
- `do.industries` - Core business primitives
- `schema.org.ai` - 800+ Schema.org types + AI
- `gs1.org.ai` - Global standards + AI
- `mdx.org.ai` - AI-native MDX

**Layer 2: Platform**
- `apis.do` - Universal RPC + AI adapters

**Layer 3: Managed Services**
- `llm.do`, `embeddings.do`, `models.do`, `sdk.do`

**Layer 4: Special Purpose**
- `402.do`, `do.com.ai`, `graphdl`

**Layer 5: Domain Packages**
- 105 auto-generated `.do` packages for all industries
