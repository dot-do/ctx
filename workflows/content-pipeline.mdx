# Content Creation Pipeline

An automated workflow orchestrating multiple AI agents to create publication-ready blog content.

## Workflow Stages

### Stage 1: Research & Planning
- **Topic Research** (5 min): Research agent gathers information and sources
- **Outline Creation** (3 min): Content writer structures the article

### Stage 2: Content Creation
- **Draft Writing** (15 min): Content writer produces first draft
- **SEO Optimization** (4 min): SEO agent optimizes for search engines

### Stage 3: Quality Assurance
- **Fact Checking** (5 min): Research agent verifies accuracy
- **Editorial Review** (5 min): Editor agent reviews quality

### Stage 4: Finalization
- **Final Revisions** (4 min): Content writer applies feedback
- **Publication** (2 min): Publishing agent deploys to CMS (requires approval)

## Agents Involved

- **Research Agent**: Information gathering and fact-checking
- **Content Writer**: Primary content creation
- **SEO Agent**: Search engine optimization
- **Editor Agent**: Quality review
- **Publishing Agent**: CMS deployment

## Success Metrics

The workflow ensures:
- ✅ Comprehensive research
- ✅ Structured, engaging content
- ✅ SEO optimization
- ✅ Fact accuracy
- ✅ Editorial quality
- ✅ Human approval
